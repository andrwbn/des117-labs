Kaskade

Biography

Kaskade, who's real name is Ryan Gary Raddon, was born on February 25, 1971 in Chicago, Illinois.

He is best known as an American DJ and record producer. His genre of music is within the progressive house and EDM field. Over the years he has released a total of 9 albums and still continues to be active today.

Kaskade has become very popular over this career. He has consistently been ranked in many lists as America's best DJ.
